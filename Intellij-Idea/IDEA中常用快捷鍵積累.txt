Intellij IDEA中常用快捷鍵積累

參考網址：
https://juejin.im/post/5a90810b6fb9a063606eefe0

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1.設置自動導包
File --> settings --> editor --> general --> auto import

勾選java下的add unambiguous imports on the fly
勾選java下的optimize imports on the fly (for current project)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.上下移動選中行的代碼

  選中行，Shift+Alt+Up or Shift+Alt+Down
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3.刪除行

  Ctrl+Y
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4.剪切行

  Ctrl+X
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5.生成代碼（構造方法，方法重寫，方法implements）

  Alt+Insert
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6.自動生成方法返回值

  Ctrl+Alt+V
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7.生成環繞代碼（如：try/catch）

  Ctrl+Alt+T
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8.類名導入全路徑包

  Alt+Enter
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
9.代碼補全

	Ctrl+Shift+Space
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
10.從接口方法跳轉到實現類對應方法

	Ctrl+Alt+左鍵
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
